# Quick Screenshot Guide for App Store Submission

## What You Need

You need 3-10 screenshots for each device size. Apple requires:

- **iPhone 6.5"**: 1284 x 2778 pixels
- **iPad Pro 12.9"**: 2048 x 2732 pixels

## How to Capture (Easiest Method)

### Option 1: Use Your Phone/Tablet

1. Open https://hexology.me/ on your iPhone and iPad
2. Take screenshots of these pages:
   - Home page (with the Jan 25th banner)
   - Game board (during a match)
   - Premium page (/premium)
   - Tournaments page (/tournaments)
   - Leaderboard page (/leaderboard)

3. AirDrop them to your Mac and upload to App Store Connect

### Option 2: Use Browser Dev Tools (Quick!)

1. Open Chrome/Edge
2. Press F12 to open DevTools
3. Click the device icon (top-left of DevTools)
4. Select "iPhone 14 Pro Max" from the dropdown
5. Navigate to each page and right-click > "Capture screenshot"
6. For iPad: Select "iPad Pro 12.9"

## Pages to Screenshot (in order of importance)

1. **Home page** - Shows launch dates and Discord link ✅
2. **Game board** - The actual hex game in action
3. **Premium page** - Plus features
4. **Tournaments** - Shows the $500 tournament
5. **Leaderboard** - Competitive aspect

## Upload to App Store Connect

1. Go to your app in App Store Connect
2. Click "Previews and Screenshots"
3. Drag and drop your images
4. Apple will auto-resize if needed

That's it! The metadata is already done in `store_assets/APP_STORE_METADATA.md`.
